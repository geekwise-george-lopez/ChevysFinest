Welcome to Chevy's Finest

 

The club was introduced by Javier and George in May of 2005. The reason for this club is to let every one know that we belong a club. That and to look cool with a big ass sticker in the back window saying that we belong to an association. A Chevrolet Club. This club is only Chevrolet Vehicle owners ONLY. Which means no GMC,  TOYOTA, DODGE, and especially FORD's. Even though Chevrolet and GMC is distributed by GM the club reads Chevy's Finest  not GM's Finest, Chevy's Finest.

In this site you will find lots of pictures of what we do. Going to car shows, cruising on the weekends and going to late night parties. There is also a link for any one that needs assistance with their computer. There is from building a computer to just fixing a computer. There is also I link here that displays the design of our sticker its simple but looks good. You can also set it as your the emblem as your background picture. Simply right click on the design and click on set as Background.

So thank you for visiting our site. Hope you enjoy it.
